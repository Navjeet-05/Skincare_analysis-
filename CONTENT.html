BASICS

Key Steps in a Skincare Routine:

Understanding Your Skin Type: The first step to building a good skincare routine is knowing your skin type.

~Normal: Balanced oil production.
~Dry: Lacks oil, feels tight, and prone to flaky patches.
~Oily: Overactive oil glands, shiny appearance, and frequent breakouts.
~Combination: A mix of oily and dry areas, often with an oily T-zone.
~Sensitive: Reacts easily to products or environmental factors, often with redness or irritation.

1.Cleansing : Remove dirt, oil, and impurities from your skin using a gentle cleanser that suits your skin type .
2.Toning : Restore your skin's pH balance and tighten pores with a toner.
3.Moisturizing : Hydrate your skin to keep it supple and prevent dryness. Choose a moisturizer that complements your skin type.
4.Sunscreen : Protect your skin from harmful UV rays by applying sunscreen with a broad-spectrum SPF of 30 or higher daily.

Factors Affecting Skin Health:
(a)Genetics: Your skin type and its tendency to age are influenced by your genetics.
(b)Lifestyle: Factors like diet, sleep, stress, and smoking can significantly impact your skin's health.
(c)Environment: Exposure to pollution, harsh weather, and excessive sunlight can damage your skin.

Additional Tips: (i)Exfoliate regularly: Remove dead skin cells to reveal smoother, brighter skin.
(ii) Drink plenty of water: Stay hydrated to maintain healthy skin.
(iii) Eat a balanced diet: Include fruits, vegetables, and whole grains in your diet for essential nutrients.
(iv) Consult a dermatologist: If you have specific skin concerns or conditions, seek professional advice.
